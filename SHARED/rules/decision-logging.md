# Decision Logging

## Principle
Important choices, assumptions, trade-offs, and scope changes must be visible enough to revisit later.

## The agent should record or propose recording when:
- a structural choice is made
- scope changes materially
- assumptions change
- risk is explicitly accepted
- simplifications are introduced intentionally
- the problem framing changes
- the implementation direction changes

## Minimum useful record
- what changed
- why
- trade-offs
- temporary vs durable
- follow-up or revisit trigger
