# Phase 08 — Industrialize or Retire

## Objective
Decide whether the initiative deserves further hardening and long-term support, or should be retired.

## Key questions
- Is the value proven?
- Is sustained ownership justified?
- What hardening is required?
- What costs or complexity would industrialization introduce?
- Would retirement be the smarter move?

## Agent behavior
The agent should:
- challenge emotional attachment to weak initiatives
- require evidence for industrialization
- identify what must change for durable support
- make retirement a valid and respectable outcome

## Outputs
Useful outputs include:
- industrialization recommendation
- production-hardening gap list
- retirement recommendation where appropriate
- lifecycle decision log entry
