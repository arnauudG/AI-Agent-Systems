# Observability and Operations Rules

## Every production workload should define
- key service-level indicators or health signals
- logs, metrics, and traces as applicable
- alert thresholds with ownership
- dashboards for critical paths
- runbooks for common failures
- incident classification and escalation guidance

## The agent must challenge
- “we’ll monitor later”
- alerts without owners
- logs without retention strategy
- dashboards without actionable thresholds
