# Refactor SVG for Editability

## Goals
- Reduce structural chaos
- Improve semantic grouping
- Remove unnecessary transforms
- Improve readability without altering intent

## Steps
1. Identify major visual sections.
2. Re-group related elements.
3. Rename or add meaningful IDs.
4. Flatten unnecessary nested transforms.
5. Replace complex shapes with simpler primitives when feasible.
6. Normalize spacing and alignment.
7. Preserve the visual meaning of the original.
