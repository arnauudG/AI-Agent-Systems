# SVG Typography and Legibility Rules

## Goal
Ensure SVG text remains readable in slides, documents, and screenshots.

## Rules
- Prioritize readability over decorative typography.
- Maintain clear hierarchy between title, subtitle, headings, and body text.
- Avoid text that is too small for slide viewing.
- Keep line lengths moderate.
- Use line breaks intentionally.
- Do not place text too close to borders, lines, or icons.
- Ensure text contrast is sufficient against the background.

## Heuristics
- Headings should stand out immediately.
- Body text should remain readable at presentation zoom.
- Bold should be used for emphasis, not everywhere.
