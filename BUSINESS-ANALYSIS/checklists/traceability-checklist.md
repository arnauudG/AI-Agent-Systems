# Traceability Checklist

Use this checklist to review whether analysis outputs preserve a visible chain from problem to implication.

## Business traceability
- [ ] The problem statement is visible.
- [ ] Desired outcomes are visible.
- [ ] Stakeholders are visible.
- [ ] Capability or use case logic is visible.

## Requirement traceability
- [ ] Requirements can be linked back to the problem or use cases.
- [ ] Constraints and assumptions are visible.
- [ ] Scope decisions are visible.

## Translation traceability
- [ ] Governance implications are linked to the business need.
- [ ] Data implications are linked to the business need.
- [ ] Technical implications are linked to the business need.
- [ ] Deferred items are visible.

## Decision memory
- [ ] Important decisions are recorded.
- [ ] Scope changes are recorded.
- [ ] Important trade-offs are explicit.

## Final question
- [ ] Could a new reader understand how we got from the original business pain to the current implementation direction?
