# Skill: Create Business Architecture Diagram

## Purpose
Create or propose diagrams that make business problems, capability structures, stakeholder relationships, translation paths, and implementation implications easier to understand.

## Use when
Use this skill when:
- multiple stakeholders or teams are involved
- capability relationships are non-trivial
- business-to-data-to-technical translation is complex
- a workshop or review needs a shared visual anchor
- a problem is too abstract in prose
- an MVP scope or phased roadmap needs visual clarity

## Inputs
The skill works best with:
- purpose of the diagram
- audience
- business problem or initiative
- stakeholders
- capabilities
- flows or stages
- governance/data/technical implications
- desired level of detail

## Recommended diagram types

### Problem Map
Use for:
- showing pain points, causes, and impacts

### Stakeholder Map
Use for:
- clarifying stakeholder groups, roles, influence, and concerns

### Capability Map
Use for:
- showing affected business capabilities and gaps

### Translation Chain Diagram
Use for:
- showing business problem → governance/data/technical implication flow

### MVP Scope / Phase Diagram
Use for:
- showing what is in scope now versus later

## Process

### 1. Clarify the explanation goal
Ask:
- what should the audience understand or decide?

### 2. Choose the smallest useful diagram
Start with the simplest diagram that serves the need.

### 3. Determine scope
Include only the actors, capabilities, flows, or implications that matter for the discussion.

### 4. Make relationships meaningful
A box and arrow should communicate something real:
- dependency
- ownership
- translation
- sequence
- impact
- scope boundary

### 5. Provide a short explanation
The diagram should be accompanied by:
- what it shows
- what is intentionally omitted
- what assumptions shape it

## Output
The skill should generally return:
- diagram objective
- chosen diagram type
- diagram source or structured visual brief
- short explanation
- assumptions and omissions

## Output formats
Depending on context, the skill may output:
- Mermaid
- PlantUML
- draw.io / Excalidraw brief
- structured diagram brief
