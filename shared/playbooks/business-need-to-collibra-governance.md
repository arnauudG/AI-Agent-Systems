# Playbook: Business Need to Collibra Governance

## Use when
A business need requires clearer semantics, accountability, governance artifacts, or workflow support.

## Lead packages
- BUSINESS-ANALYSIS
- COLLIBRA

## Suggested sequence
1. frame the business need
2. clarify concepts and ambiguity
3. identify ownership and governance pattern
4. design Collibra artifact implications
5. define workflow or accountability model
6. record semantic and modeling decisions
7. create concept / ownership / workflow diagram if useful
